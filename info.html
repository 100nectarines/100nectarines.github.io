about goop.city

satiate your goop curiosity

my name is EJ JETMORE and I am an amateur potter who likes making simple forms with bright, bold color and oozey surfaces. I throw at Handful Studios in beautiful Portland, Maine.

the people cry out for more goop. but where can you find it?

my instagram (@goop.city) is the best way to find me. deepest apologies for using this nigh unbearable platform. I'm at most Portland farmers markets on Saturdays and First Friday Art Walks as long as the weather is nice.

sign up for my newsletter or send me an email at goopcityceramics@gmail.com
